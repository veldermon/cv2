Alexander Velderman

From Toronto, Canada, and based in London, England. His practise centers around the image, experimental media, digital humanities, and human interaction.
He holds an Honours Bachelor of Arts from the Ontario College of Art and Design University (OCADU), and is an MA candidate at Goldsmiths University of London in Computational Arts.
While Alexander has a background in image and video arts, his work is interdisciplinary and encompasses techniques such as, 3D rendering, programming, and video.
His work has been featured in shows in London, Toronto, and Mexico City.
